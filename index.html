A Daily Execution System
T
Think
Before you move, gain clarity. Most mistakes happen in the thinking stage — or the absence of it. Think before you act.
→ What do I need to think clearly about today?
P
Plan
A plan converts thought into direction. Without it, effort scatters. Choose your top 3 actions. Not ten — three that truly matter.
→ What are my 3 most important actions today?
A
Act
Execution is where frameworks die. Act before you feel ready. Discipline carries you when motivation doesn't show up.
→ What did I actually do today?
T
Track
Tracking closes the loop. The person who tracks is the person who compounds — day after day, week after week.
→ What went well? What didn't? What changes tomorrow?
